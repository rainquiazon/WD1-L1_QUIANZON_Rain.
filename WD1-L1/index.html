The rain fell slow on the empty road.
Yamada walked alone, his jacket heavy with water.
He did not mind.
Silence was his friend tonight.
But at least the moon is bright.

He saw a small cat under a bench.
Its fur was wet, its eyes wide.
Yamada stopped and knelt.
He offered his hand.
The cat stayed, watching.

Trust takes time.
He sat beside it, the rain kept falling.
He shared his bread.
The cat ate.
In that quiet street, two lonely lives found warmth. This proved that warmth does not only come from a fire.
